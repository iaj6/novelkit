## The Decision (structural note for drafting)

Elena's resolution — the "something else" of the brief — is: she documents the signal with complete scientific rigor, preserves all three copies intact, writes the full methodology in the official log, and does not transmit. She waits to hand the finding to a human being she trusts before it enters the apparatus. It is neither institutional disclosure nor personal suppression nor destruction. It is the act of a scientist who has decided the finding deserves one more act of care before the world gets it. Whether this is right is not resolved. The ending is what is on her desk when the team walks in: the official log open to page 47, the labeled tape in the archive box, the printout filed and annotated in her handwriting. She has done the science. She is handing it over.
