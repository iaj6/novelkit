# Story Arc

One tight line per drafted chapter, in chronological order.

- **01-the-eleven-bell** — Three apprentices meet in disused Classroom 2-F, argue through the heist plan, acknowledge their asymmetric stakes, and step into the dark corridor together as the eleven-bell patrol passes.
- **02-the-archive** — The three execute the ward-gap sequence, recover Emric's notebook, and Mira finds the Conclave Registry — all three names in it — which she pockets in silence as an unscheduled patrol enters the floor above.
- **03-the-passage** — Mira routes the three through the service passage, tells them about the Registry, survives the argument over burning it, and tucks it inside her coat as the midnight bell rings mid-step.
