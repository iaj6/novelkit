# Chapter Map — Thornwick (working title)

## Summary

Three second-year apprentices break into the Thornwick archive during the fifty-minute window between the eleven-bell and midnight patrols. They go in for Emric's confiscated notebook. They find the Conclave Registry. Mira reads it, understands what it means, and has to decide what to do with that knowledge before the midnight bell rings.

## Structural spine

This is a three-act heist in three chapters. Each chapter is one location, one scene, no subplots.

**The plan's key mechanics (established in Ch 1):**
- **Mira** operates the ward-interaction gap sequence
- **Cass** picks the mechanical padlock with two modified iron pins
- **Emric** retrieves the notebook (he knows Thornwick's filing logic)
- Rule: nobody moves without Mira's signal

**The plan's specific failure (occurs in Ch 2):**
Emric counts to forty-five instead of sixty during the ward-gap window (he counts fast when scared). His foot crosses the trip-wire ward's secondary sweep. A half-alert — not a full alarm, but a "query pulse" — transmits to the patrol system and dispatches Prefect Hale in thirty minutes. The three do not know this happened. They discover the compressed window only when they hear her lantern chain in the corridor above.

**The complication that exceeds the plan (Ch 2):**
They came for one notebook. Mira finds the Conclave Registry. The heist's object changes mid-chapter.

**The open ending (Ch 3):**
Mira tucks the Registry inside her coat and starts climbing the staircase as the midnight bell rings. The hearing is in five hours. The consequences are not shown.
