## 02-east

## 03-dereliction

Radwick says: "Ash. I heard you got derelicted. Heard the whole story, too." Corvin's response: "The road's clear. Walk off it." They go. Corvin watches the scrubline for sixty seconds. The aftermath: two hundred meters of silence with the specific texture of people deciding whether to ask. He notices Sela held force-push prepared the entire fight and chose not to release it — the headache is real regardless. Nobody asks him anything at camp. The silence behind him is different from the silence before.

## 06-what-the-empire-paved
