# Story Arc

One tight line per drafted chapter, in chronological order.

- **01-the-worst-job-on-the-board** — Four strangers — Sela, Corvin, Evren, and Agda — independently take the only job nobody else will touch and sign a contract to find Maret Ashford's missing brother in the Hollowback.
- **02-east** — The party travels east across two days; Evren reads his companions and keeps his secret, and in one night-watch moment the joke stops and the real reason surfaces — briefly — before he shuts it down.
- **03-dereliction** — A road ambush by deserters ends when a former soldier named Radwick says "I heard you got derelicted" in front of the whole party; Corvin says nothing, and the silence that follows is different from any silence before it.
- **04-aftertaste** — Sela pays the held-spell cost in the ambush's quiet aftermath; Agda says "leave it" with the weight of someone who already knows; Corvin says "good decision" and nothing else.
- **05-charter** — Agda invokes dwarven customary law to get the party past a hill-dwarf charter holder's checkpoint; the holder's parting warning in Dwarven changes what the waystation is.
- **06-what-the-empire-paved** — Sela finds the Vethic road still flat under three centuries of forest floor; nobody makes a joke; Evren says *she* and stops; something moves at the camp edge with intention.
- **07-watch** — Evren keeps watch alone with Lira's name in his head; Agda wakes, doesn't push, falls asleep beside him; at dawn, Ren's camp and a Vethic notation Evren reads too fluently.
- **08-the-antechamber** — The party cracks the Eastmark Waystation's puzzle-trapped antechamber — pressure plates, a combination lock, total darkness — and inside the lower corridor something in the far dark makes a sound too regular to be water.
- **09-interior** — Evren leads the party through the garrison and storage rooms, finds Ren's workspace, reads the maps fluently for the third time, and learns the containment chamber has something still in it — then the sound is closer.
- **10-found** — Ren is found alive and argumentative in a Vethic archive three levels down; he shows the party the containment chamber and the human-shaped thing inside it, ending the contract's simplicity.
- **11-the-thing-the-empire-kept** — Ren explains the Hollowed One — a Vethic experimental subject suspended at the threshold for three centuries — and Agda calls Duhr, who answers: a wound held open, not at rest.
- **12-what-we-do-now** — The party argues about the knowledge and arrives at a plan: reseal the containment mechanism themselves, using the skills they have between them, before leaving the waystation with Ren.
- **13-the-hollowed** — The party reseals the Hollowed One while Corvin holds the draw's path; Agda finishes the invocation on her knees; Sela pushes past threshold and goes down; Corvin carries her out.
- **14-threshold** — The party reverses the antechamber and escapes; Agda says *halveth* at the threshold and Duhr closes the door; outside, she stands beside Evren until he uncrosses his arms.
- **15-the-road-tells-you-things** — Four days of road compressed into relationship beats: the five-person decision to keep the knowledge, Sela naming the shape of Corvin's discharge, and a near-moment that ends with "yeah" and the road still clear.
- **16-thorngate** — Sela delivers Ren to Maret, collects thirty-seven marks, says goodbye to each of them in turn, and walks home beside Corvin — the contract closed; everything else remains open.
