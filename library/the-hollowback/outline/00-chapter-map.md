## Continuity Anchors (per-chapter)
- Magic cost is paid every time Sela casts. Tracked across Chs 8, 13.
- Corvin's discharge is never fully explained; first alluded to in Ch 3, pressed on in Ch 4, Agda's guess confirmed to herself in Ch 11.
- Evren's hidden reason surfaces in his interiority (Chs 2, 7, 9) but is never disclosed aloud.
- Duhr answers Agda in Chs 5, 11, 14.
- The party possesses the knowledge of the waystation's contents at the end of Ch 12; this does not change through Ch 16.
